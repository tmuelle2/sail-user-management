SAIL strives to connect people to people which is why Friendship Connect was created. Friendship Connect’s online portal and live events provides a forum for friendships to develop, roommates to emerge and shared housing solutions to evolve. SAIL hosts Friendship Connect Events every other month (see calendar of events for details) where families and individuals are invited to participate.

In order to sign up for Friendship Connect’s online portal, you will need to become a SAIL member. Please follow this link to Join SAIL. Once you are a SAIL member, follow this link to Login.
