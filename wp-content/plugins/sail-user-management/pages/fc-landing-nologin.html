SAIL believes in connecting people to people, so we developed Friendship Connect to help you. Whether you are looking to find friends or looking for a roommate, Friendship Connect is a way to start your journey and find your crew.

In order to sign up for Friendship Connect you will need to become a SAIL member first. Please follow this link to Join SAIL. If you are already a SAIL member please follow this link to Login.

Location: Troy, Michigan
